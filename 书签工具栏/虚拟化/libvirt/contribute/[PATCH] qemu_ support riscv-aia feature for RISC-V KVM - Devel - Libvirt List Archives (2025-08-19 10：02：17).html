[PATCH] qemu: support riscv-aia feature for RISC-V KVM

BillXiang
星期五, 15 八月 2025 星期五, 15 八月 '25
07:41
riscv-aia feature was introduced in qemu-8.2 to specify the KVM AIA mode. The "riscv-aia" parameter is passed along with --accel in QEMU command-line. 1) "riscv-aia=emul": IMSIC is emulated by hypervisor 2) "riscv-aia=hwaccel": use hardware guest IMSIC 3) "riscv-aia=auto": use the hardware guest IMSICs whenever available otherwise we fallback to software emulation. This patch add the corresponding feature named 'riscv-aia'.
