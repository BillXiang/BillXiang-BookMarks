From: xiangwencheng <xiangwencheng@lanxincomputing.com> riscv-aia feature was introduced in qemu-8.2 to specify the KVM AIA mode. The "riscv-aia" parameter is passed along with -accel in QEMU command-line. 1) "riscv-aia=emul": IMSIC is emulated by hypervisor 2) "riscv-aia=hwaccel": use hardware guest IMSIC 3) "riscv-aia=auto": use the hardware guest IMSICs whenever available otherwise we fallback to software emulation. This patch add the corresponding feature named 'riscv-aia'.
