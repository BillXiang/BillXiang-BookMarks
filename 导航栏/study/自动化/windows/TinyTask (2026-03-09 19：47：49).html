Simple + Fast + Free:
Automation for Everyone
 

TinyTask is a minimalist PC automation app you can use to record and repeat actions. As the name implies, it's unbelievably small (only 36KB!), ultra compact, and 100% portable.
No scripting is necessary - just press record, then play!
